Review Response Best Practices

For Positive Reviews

  • • Thank the customer by name
  • • Mention specific details they appreciated
  • • Invite them to book again
  • • Keep responses warm and personal

For Critical Reviews

  • • Stay professional and empathetic
  • • Acknowledge their concerns
  • • Offer to discuss privately
  • • Show commitment to improvement
Response Time Matters
Responding to reviews within 24-48 hours shows professionalism and helps build trust with potential customers.
