Declaração de Acessibilidade

Effective date: [DD Month YYYY]

1 – Our Commitment

Zela Serviços Domésticos, Lda. ("Zela") is committed to making zela.co.ao and all future mobile apps usable by everyone, including people with disabilities. We aim to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 level AA.

2 – Measures We Take

  • • Semantic HTML5 and ARIA landmarks.
  • • Keyboard-navigable menus, forms and carousels.
  • • Sufficient colour-contrast ratios (≥ 4.5:1).
  • • Text alternatives for images, icons and video.
  • • Focus-visible styles and logical tab order.
  • • Captions on marketing videos and transcripts on request.
  • • Compatibility testing with assistive technologies such as NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver and TalkBack.
  • • Monthly automated scans plus manual audits every quarter.

3 – Conformance Status

As of the effective date this site partially conforms to WCAG 2.1 AA. Known limitations are listed in §4 and remediation is scheduled within 90 days.

4 – Known Issues & Roadmap

Issue Impact Target fix
Some dynamic Toast messages lack aria-live regions Screen-reader users may miss notifications 30 days
Colour contrast on older blog images Low-vision users 60 days
Drag-scroll carousel not keyboard-operable Keyboard-only users 90 days

Zela self-evaluates using Axe DevTools, Lighthouse, and manual checks by users of screen readers and high-contrast modes.
