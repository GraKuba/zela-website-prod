Our Story

Zela was born in Luanda out of a simple insight: busy families deserve spotless homes without the hassle of endless phone calls, cash negotiations, or no-shows.

Inspired by the Portuguese verb zelar—to care for and pay close attention—we set out to bring professionalism and dignity to Angola's household-services market.

What started as a small team coordinating weekend cleans has grown into a nationwide platform connecting thousands of households with trusted domestic workers, gardeners, handymen, and more.
