4. Managing Cookies

You can:

  • • Adjust browser settings to refuse or delete cookies.
  • • Use our on-site Cookie Settings panel (bottom-left icon) to toggle non-essential cookies.

⚠️ Rejecting cookies may limit certain features (e.g., remembering your language).

5. Do Not Track
