4. How We Use It

  • • Process bookings and send confirmations.
  • • Match you with vetted providers based on skills, distance, and rating.
  • • Facilitate payments via Paystack or PayPal.
  • • Improve the platform through analytics and A/B testing.
  • • Send service or marketing messages (opt-out anytime).
  • • Fulfil legal obligations, e.g., invoices and anti-fraud checks.

5. Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell data. We share it only with:

  • • Service providers you book, limited to job-relevant details.
  • • Payment processors (Paystack, PayPal) for secure transactions.
  • • Cloud & analytics vendors (e.g., Cloudflare, Google Analytics) under SCCs or APD-approved safeguards.
  • • Authorities where required by law or court order.

6. International Transfers

Servers may sit outside Angola (e.g., EU). We use Standard Contractual Clauses and ISO-27001-certified providers to keep your data safe.

7. Retention

Bookings: 6 years (tax law). Payments: 10 years (audits). Marketing consents: until you withdraw. Candidate data: 2 years after last activity.

8. Your Rights

  • • Access, rectify, erase, or port your data.
  • • Restrict or object to processing.
  • • Withdraw consent for marketing at any time.
  • • File a complaint with the Angolan Data Protection Agency (APD).

Request these via DPO Email; we reply within 30 days.

9. Security

TLS 1.3 encryption, role-based access, regular penetration tests, and real-time anomaly detection.

10. Children

Zela is not intended for users under 16. We delete any such data on discovery.

11. Changes

We may update this Policy; we'll post the new version and, for material changes, e-mail registered users 7 days in advance.
